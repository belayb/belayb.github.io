Research Interests

I am interested in a wide range of research topics most of which involve the development, evaluation, or implementation of statistical models that allow for a full exploration of longitudinal and/or complex time - to - event data. I am also involved in open source software development with the aim of making novel statistical methods accessible to researchers with limited programming skills (see Open-source tools ).

Current Main Research Interests

  • Bayesian methods
  • Variable and structure selection
  • Model averaging
  • Longitudinal/Multilevel modelling
  • Causal inference
